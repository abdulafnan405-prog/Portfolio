Hi, I’m Abdul Afnan

Pursuing Electronics and comunication Engineering

I am Abdul Afnan, an ECE student who enjoys turning ideas into practical solutions. My interest lies where electronics meets software, whether it's understanding how systems work or building something useful from scratch. I believe skills grow faster through projects than through theory alone, so I focus on learning by creating. This portfolio is a collection of my work, progress, and the challenges that continue to shape my journey

What attracts me to Data Science is not just the technology itself, but the ability to find patterns where others see only information. While exploring different skills and career paths, I realized that curiosity is one of my strongest traits, and Data Science gives me endless opportunities to use it. Every dataset feels like a puzzle waiting to be solved, and every project teaches me a new way of thinking. My goal is to keep learning, keep building, and gradually transform that curiosity into expertise.
